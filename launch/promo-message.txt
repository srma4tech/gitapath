Launching today on Product Hunt: GitaPath

GitaPath is a free, installable PWA for daily Bhagavad Gita practice.
- 1 verse per day from all 700 verses
- Sanskrit + transliteration
- Hindi and English meanings
- Reflection + streak tracking
- Works offline after first load
