# Product Hunt Description - GitaPath

## Tagline
Daily Bhagavad Gita verse, reflection, and spiritual streak tracking.

## Short Description
GitaPath helps you read one Bhagavad Gita verse daily with Sanskrit, transliteration, Hindi and English meaning, plus reflection and streak milestones.

## Full Description
GitaPath is a calm, focused Progressive Web App for daily Bhagavad Gita practice.

What you get:
- One verse every day from a complete local 700-verse dataset
- Sanskrit text, transliteration, Hindi meaning, and English meaning
- Guided reflection paragraph for practical daily integration
- Streak tracking with milestones at day 7, 21, and 108
- Share-ready verse cards
- Offline mode after first load (PWA)

Why it is different:
- No account required
- No ads, no backend dependency
- Mobile-first and installable from browser
- Works on GitHub Pages with strong performance

Perfect for:
- Students and professionals who want a short daily spiritual anchor
- Readers building a steady scripture habit
- Anyone looking for wisdom + consistency, not content overload
