# Product Hunt Launch Checklist - GitaPath

## Before Launch (2-7 days earlier)
- Finalize product URL: `https://YOUR_GITHUB_USERNAME.github.io/gitapath/`
- Verify app works on mobile and desktop
- Verify offline mode after first load
- Prepare at least 4 visuals:
  - App home screen
  - Daily verse view
  - Share card
  - Streak milestone view
- Prepare maker profile and links
- Draft Product Hunt description (`product-hunt-description.md`)
- Draft first maker comment (`first-comment.md`)
- Prepare outreach list of close supporters

## Product Hunt Listing Setup
- Product name: `GitaPath`
- Tagline: one clear line about daily verse + streak
- Description: copy from prepared markdown
- Pricing: `Free`
- Topics: `Productivity`, `Education`, `Spirituality` (or closest fit)
- Maker comment: paste and personalize from template

## Launch Day
- Publish the listing
- Post first maker comment in first 15 minutes
- Share launch link with warm audience
- Reply to every comment quickly and thoughtfully
- Capture top feedback points in one doc

## Post Launch (24-72 hours)
- Update README with “As seen on Product Hunt” if relevant
- Ship top 1-2 feedback fixes quickly
- Post update with what was improved
- Thank supporters personally

## Scheduling Note
- Product Hunt launches can be **scheduled in advance**.
- Schedule early, then spend launch day on engagement and feedback, not setup.
